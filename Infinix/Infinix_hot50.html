Infinix hot50

The Infinix Hot 50 features a 6.7-inch IPS LCD display with 120Hz refresh rate and 720 x 1600 pixels resolution. Powered by a Mediatek Dimensity 6300 or MediaTek Helio G100 processor, it comes with 4GB or 8GB RAM and 128GB internal storage, expandable via microSD card. The phone has a dual-camera setup with a 48MP primary sensor and 2MP depth sensor, and an 8MP front camera. It packs a 5000mAh battery with 18W fast charging and runs on Android 14 with XOS 14.5. Additional features include a side-mounted fingerprint sensor and face unlock.
