Samsung A10

The Samsung Galaxy A10 features a 6.2-inch HD+ Infinity-V display with 720 x 1520 pixels resolution. Powered by an Octa-core Exynos 7884 processor and 2GB/3GB RAM, it has a single 13MP rear camera and 5MP front camera. The phone has a 3400mAh battery and runs on Android 9.0 (Pie) with One UI. It comes in various colors with storage options of 32GB, expandable via microSD card.
