Samsung S23

The Samsung Galaxy S23 features a 6.1-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display with 120Hz refresh rate and 1080 x 2220 pixels resolution. Powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 processor and up to 16GB RAM, it offers a triple-camera setup with 50MP primary, 12MP front camera, and supports 200MP camera. The phone has a 3900mAh battery with 25W fast charging and wireless charging, runs on Android 13 with One UI 5.1, and comes in various colors with storage options ranging from 128GB to 512GB.
