Infinix hot50 pro

The Infinix Hot 50 Pro features a 6.78-inch AMOLED display with 1080 x 2436 pixels resolution and 120Hz refresh rate. Powered by a MediaTek Helio G100 processor, it comes with 8GB RAM and 128GB internal storage, expandable via microSD card. The phone has a triple-camera setup with a 50MP primary sensor, 2MP depth sensor, and an unspecified third camera, along with an 8MP front camera. It packs a 5000mAh battery with 33W fast charging and runs on Android 14 with XOS 14.5.
