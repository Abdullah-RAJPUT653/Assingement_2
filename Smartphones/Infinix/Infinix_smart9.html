Infinix smart9

The Infinix Smart 9 features a 6.6-inch HD+ display with a resolution of 720 x 1612 pixels and a 90Hz refresh rate. It is powered by an octa-core processor and comes with 4GB of RAM and 64GB of internal storage, expandable via microSD card. The phone has a dual-camera setup on the rear, consisting of a 13MP primary sensor and an AI lens, along with a dual flash. For selfies, it sports a 5MP front camera with an LED flash. The device runs on XOS 14 based on Android 14 Go Edition. It is fueled by a 5000mAh battery with support for 10W charging and features a side-mounted fingerprint sensor. Connectivity options include 4G VoLTE, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 5.0, GPS, and a USB-C port [2].
