Iphone 14 pro max

The iPhone 14 Pro Max is a powerful smartphone with impressive specs. It features a 6.7-inch LTPO Super Retina XDR OLED display with a resolution of 1290 x 2796 pixels, 120Hz refresh rate, and HDR10 support. Powered by the Apple A16 Bionic chip, it has a hexa-core CPU and a 5-core GPU, making it ideal for demanding tasks. The device comes in various storage options, including 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, and 1TB, paired with 6GB of RAM. The camera system is notable, with a quad-camera setup on the back, including a 48MP main camera, 12MP telephoto lens, and 12MP ultra-wide lens, along with a TOF 3D LiDAR scanner for depth sensing. The front camera is a 12MP sensor with Face ID capabilities. It supports 4K video recording at up to 60fps and has features like Night mode, Portrait mode, and cinematic video stabilization. The iPhone 14 Pro Max has a non-removable 4323mAh battery, supporting fast charging, wireless charging, and USB Power Delivery 2.0. It's rated IP68 for dust and water resistance, up to 6 meters for 30 minutes. Other key features include Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.3, and support for 5G networks ¹ ².
