Samsung S24

The Samsung Galaxy S24 features a 6.2-inch Dynamic LTPO AMOLED 2X display with 120Hz refresh rate and 1080 x 2340 pixels resolution. Powered by a Deca-core Exynos 2400 processor and 8GB RAM, it offers a triple-camera setup with 50MP primary, 10MP telephoto, and 12MP ultra-wide lenses. The phone has a 4000mAh battery with 25W fast charging and wireless charging, runs on Android 14 with One UI 6.1, and comes in various colors with 128GB to 512GB storage options.
